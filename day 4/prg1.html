City Engineering College (CEC) is an engineering college located in Bangalore, India. It was established in 2001 and is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Mechanical, Civil, and Information Science Engineering.
